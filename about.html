Our Mission

Get Back to Nature

In the Rat Race of life, humans have become more out of touch with nature. However, the benefits to human life that being in Nature has is immeasurable.

Create Personal Sustainability

Even though at a personal level we are not responsible for, nor can we solve the Climate Crisis, we can create personal sustainability to ease the burden on nature.

Promote Climate Equity

Climate Equity ensures the just distribution of the benefits of climate protection efforts and alleviates unequal burdens created by climate change.

Heal Our Planet

We must start to heal our Planet now. Before it is too late. The fact is, the world won't be able to meet its climate change goals without our input. Nature can help heal our planet, if we let it.

Our Philosophy

Help every person take small steps towards a better and more sustainable future
